A Guide to the Seven Chakras

According to ancient Indian philosophy, our body contains seven main energy centers called Chakras. When these are balanced, we experience health and happiness.

1. Root Chakra (Muladhara)

Located at the base of the spine, it represents stability, safety, and grounding. Color: Red.

2. Sacral Chakra (Svadhisthana)

Located below the navel, it governs creativity, emotion, and sexuality. Color: Orange.

3. Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura)

Located in the stomach area, it is the center of personal power and confidence. Color: Yellow.

4. Heart Chakra (Anahata)

Located in the center of the chest, it represents love, compassion, and connection. Color: Green.

5. Throat Chakra (Vishuddha)

Located in the throat, it governs communication and truth. Color: Blue.

6. Third Eye Chakra (Ajna)

Located between the eyebrows, it is the center of intuition and insight. Color: Indigo.

7. Crown Chakra (Sahasrara)

Located at the top of the head, it connects us to the divine and spiritual consciousness. Color: Violet/White.

Peace. Balance. Strength. Your journey to wellness starts here.
