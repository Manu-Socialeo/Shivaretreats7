Top Benefits of a 200 Hour Yoga TTC

Embarking on a 200 Hour Yoga Teacher Training Course (TTC) is a transformative decision. Whether you aim to become a certified teacher or simply wish to deepen your personal practice, Goa offers the perfect environment for this journey.

1. Deepen Your Personal Practice

A TTC isn't just about teaching others; it's about understanding your own body and mind. You'll dive deep into Asanas, Pranayama, and Meditation, refining your alignment and technique under expert guidance.

2. Understand Yoga Philosophy

Yoga is more than exercise. In our 200 Hour course, you explore the Yoga Sutras of Patanjali, learn about the Eight Limbs of Yoga, and understand how to apply these ancient ethical principles to modern life.

3. Gain Confidence to Teach

Through practical teaching labs, you will find your unique voice. You'll learn how to sequence classes, adjust students safely, and lead a class with confidence and compassion.

4. Connect with a Global Community

Goa attracts yoga enthusiasts from all over the world. You'll make lifelong friends and join a supportive sangha (community) that spans across continents.
