Ayurveda & Yoga: The Sister Sciences

Yoga and Ayurveda are two sides of the same coin. While Yoga focuses on the harmonization of mind and spirit, Ayurveda focuses on the health and balance of the physical body. Together, they offer a complete system for well-being.

Understanding Your Dosha

Ayurveda teaches that we are all made of five elements (Ether, Air, Fire, Water, Earth), which combine to form three Doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha. Understanding your dominant Dosha helps you tailor your diet and lifestyle for optimal health.

Yoga for Your Dosha

Did you know that your yoga practice can be adjusted to balance your Dosha?
- Vata types benefit from grounding, slow Hatha yoga.
- Pitta types benefit from cooling, relaxing practices.
- Kapha types benefit from stimulating, dynamic flows.

Healing Through Food

"Food is thy medicine." Ayurveda emphasizes eating fresh, seasonal, and sattvic foods that align with your constitution. This approach prevents disease and promotes clarity of mind, which supports your meditation practice.
