Preparing for Your First Yoga TTC

Signing up for a Yoga Teacher Training Course is exciting, but it can also be nerve-wracking. Here are some tips to help you prepare for this life-changing experience.

Physical Preparation

You don't need to be able to do a handstand, but building some stamina is helpful. Try to practice yoga consistently 3-4 times a week before you arrive. This will help your body adapt to the intensive schedule of the course.

Mental Preparation

Come with an open mind. A TTC is not just about physical exercise; it's a journey into self-discovery. Be ready to unlearn old habits and embrace new perspectives.

What to Study?

While we cover everything from scratch, reading a bit about "The Yoga Sutras of Patanjali" or "Anatomy of Yoga" can give you a head start.

Set an Intention

Why are you doing this course? Is it to teach? To heal? To find balance? Setting a sankalpa (intention) will guide you through the challenging moments of the training.

Peace. Balance. Strength. Your journey to wellness starts here.
