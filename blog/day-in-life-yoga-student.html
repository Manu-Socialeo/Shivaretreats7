A Day in the Life of a Yoga Student

Life during a Yoga Teacher Training is structured, intense, yet incredibly rewarding. Here is a glimpse of what your daily schedule might look like at Shiva Yoga Goa.

06:30 AM – Pranayama & Meditation

We start the day with the rising sun. Learning to control the breath and still the mind sets the tone for the rest of the day.

08:00 AM – Asana Practice (Ashtanga/Vinyasa)

A dynamic practice to wake up the body, build strength, and sweat out toxins.

09:30 AM – Breakfast

A healthy, vegetarian breakfast to nourish the body. Think fresh fruits, porridge, herbal tea, and local Indian delicacies.

11:00 AM – Philosophy / Anatomy

Time to engage the mind. We study ancient texts or learn about the mechanics of the human body.

01:00 PM – Lunch & Rest

A sattvic lunch followed by some downtime. You can study, nap, or take a quick dip in the sea.

04:00 PM – Asana Practice (Hatha/Alignment)

A slower, more detailed practice focusing on correct alignment and teaching methodology.

07:00 PM – Dinner & Free Time

Enjoy a communal dinner and share stories with your fellow students before retiring early for a good night's sleep.

Peace. Balance. Strength. Your journey to wellness starts here.
