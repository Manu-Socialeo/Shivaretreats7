Important Information

What to Pack

  • Comfortable yoga clothes (leggings, tank tops, loose pants).
  • White clothes for the Opening and Closing ceremonies.
  • Personal toiletries (shampoo, soap, sunscreen, insect repellent).
  • A notebook and pens for taking notes during theory classes.
  • Reuseable water bottle.
  • Any personal medication you may require.
  • Sandals or flip-flops for easy movement.

Visa & Documents

International students require a valid Indian Visa. You can apply for a Tourist Visa or E-Visa online before your travel. Please ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months.

Arrival & Transfer

The nearest airport is Manohar International Airport (MOPA) or Dabolim Airport (GOI). We can arrange a taxi transfer for you from the airport directly to the school at an additional cost. Please share your flight details with us in advance.

Weather

Goa has a tropical climate. November to March is the peak season with pleasant weather. April and May can be warm, while June to September is the monsoon season, which is lush green and beautiful but wet.
