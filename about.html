About Shiva Yoga Goa

Authentic Yoga Teacher Training in the heart of Arambol

Our Mission

Shiva Yoga Goa is dedicated to spreading the ancient wisdom of yoga in a modern, accessible format. Located in the serene beach town of Arambol, we offer a sanctuary for students to deepen their practice, disconnect from the stresses of daily life, and reconnect with their true selves.

The Goa Experience

Training with us is more than just a course; it's an immersion into the vibrant culture of Goa. Our students enjoy:

  • Daily practice in our open-air shala
  • Sunset beach walks and meditation
  • Weekly excursions to local markets and temples
  • A supportive community of like-minded yogis

Peace. Balance. Strength. Your journey to wellness starts here.
