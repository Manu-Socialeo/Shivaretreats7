Hatha Yoga

Balance your body and mind through traditional practice.

Course Overview

Hatha Yoga is a traditional practice that focuses on physical postures (asanas) and breath control (pranayama). It is designed to align your body, skin, muscles, and bones.

Our Hatha Yoga classes move at a slower pace, allowing you to focus on the structure of each pose and hold it for longer periods. This helps to build strength and flexibility while calming the mind.

Key Focus Areas

  • Foundational Asanas
  • Proper Alignment & Adjustment
  • Pranayama (Breathing Exercises)
  • Relaxation Techniques
  • Building Core Strength
  • Mindfulness & Meditation
  • Shatkarma (Cleansing)
