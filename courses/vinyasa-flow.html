Vinyasa Flow Yoga

Connect breath with movement in a dynamic flow.

Course Overview

Vinyasa Flow is a dynamic style of yoga that links breath with movement. The word "Vinyasa" means "to place in a special way," and in this practice, we move fluidly from one pose to the next.

This course is designed to build strength, flexibility, and mindfulness. You will learn to synchronize your breath with a continuous flow of postures.

What You Will Learn

  • Sun Salutations A & B
  • Fluid transitions between poses
  • Ujjayi Breath techniques
  • Core strengthening sequences
  • Balancing postures
  • Inversions and arm balances
  • Creative sequencing
