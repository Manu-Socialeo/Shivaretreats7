28 Days - 250 Hrs Course

An intensive deep-dive into advanced yogic studies.

Beyond the 200 Hours

Designed for dedicated practitioners, our 250-hour course spans 28 days and bridges the gap between the 200-hour and 300-hour trainings. This course offers extra contact hours, more in-depth philosophy, and advanced asana workshops.

With an extended duration, you have more time to integrate the teachings, refine your personal practice, and develop your unique voice as a teacher.

Course Highlights

  • Advanced Asana Clinics
  • Extended Philosophy Lectures
  • Therapeutic Yoga Applications
  • Teaching Methodology Practicums
  • Mudra, Banda & Kriya Workshops
  • Community Teaching Projects
  • 250-hour Certification
